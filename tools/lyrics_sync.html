Guide

Normal Line

Press S exactly when singing starts. Press E when the last sung word ends. The words are spread inside that start-to-end range.

No Gap

If the next line begins immediately, press Space. That ends the current line and starts the next line at the same time.

Instrumental Gap

Press E when the previous line finishes. Wait through the music. Press S when the next line starts.
